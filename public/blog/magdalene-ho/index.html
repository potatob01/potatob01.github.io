Magdalene Ho and Competition in Music

Comments on the 2025 Cliburn Competition

Jun 10, 2025
1 minute read

If you’ve been following the Seventeenth Van Cliburn International Piano Competition, you’ve almost certainly heard the public outrage regarding Magdalene Ho’s elimination after the preliminary round. Being possibly the most liked player from the preliminary round, fans on every social media platform imaginable expressed their shock and disappointment after she was cut. Ben Laude from Tonebase even made a video sharing his “concern” on the matter, garnering tens of thousands of views in a few days. It’s not without reason. You can watch her performance here and form your own opinions, but you can see why she stood out to the audience. Not only does she have clear musical ideas, but she’s able to express them with such persuasiveness through a plethora of different colors and tones.
